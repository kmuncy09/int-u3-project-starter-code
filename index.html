Jonathan's Trip

Summer vacation has just begun. Jonathan's family spontaneously decides to go on a day trip to kickstart the summer. Where should they go?

Jonathan's family is going to St. Louis! They pack up the car and head west. On the interstate, Jonathan plays the license plate game with his sister Jenny. Jenny wins with California. About halfway, they stop at a gas station. Jonathan and Jenny stock up on soft drinks, chips, and candy bars for their trip. Eventually, the car goes up a hill and the city is in view. Where should they go first?

Jonathan's family decides to go to Indianapolis! They gather what they need for the day and get on the road. Jonathan and his sister Jenny play I-spy on the ride. The car is running low on gas a couple miles from Indy, so the family stops at a gas station. Jonathan and Jenny grab Icees and trail mix for their journey. Where should they go first?

Finally, the family arrives at the Gateway Arch. They first take some pictures outside. Then, they enter the underground museum. After exploring the museum some, Jonathan, Jenny, and their dad get in line for the elevator. Their mom is afraid of heights, so she stays underground and learns more in the museum. The rest of the family waits for an hour to go up the elevator. They go up and admire the city below. When they get back down, they rejoin their mom, excited. They leave the museum. Where should they go next?

The family arrives at the City Museum. They go in and by tickets. Then, they set off to have fun and agree to meet back at noon if they get separated. Jonathan, Jenny, their dad, and their mom wander through tunnels, slides, staircase, and more. Filled with joy, they meet back at noon. The fmaily heads out to lunch. They find a brewery where they have great burgers. Where should they go next?

Jonathan and his family approach the Indianapolis Zoo. They enter the zoo and pay for tickets. They visit the aquatic animals first. Next, they head to the desert section. Jonathan enjoyed the Giant Tortoise exhibit. Next came the plains. The mom's favorite exhibit, the cheetah, was in this section. The birds were next. The family then stopped for lunch before visiting the forest area. Jenny's favorite animal, the macaw, was in that section. The dad could not decide his favorite. The forest was the last section, so the family left after that. Where shoud they go next?

The family drove into the center of downtown Indy. After parking, they walked to the Soldiers and Sailors Monument. The family took several pictures and gazed at the architecture. Next, they walked around downtown, going into a couple stores. They ate at Cafe Patachou for lunch. Then they were ready for the next stop. Where should they go next?
