Fun Day Trip

Summer vacation has just begun. Your family spontaneously decides to go on a day trip to kickstart the summer. Where should you go?

Your family is going to St. Louis! You pack up the car and head west. On the interstate, you play the license plate game with your sibling. Your sibling wins with California. About halfway, your family stops at a gas station. You and your sibling stock up on soft drinks, chips, and candy bars for your trip. Eventually, the car goes up a hill and the city is in view. Where should you go first?

Your family decides to go to Indianapolis! You gather what you need for the day and get on the road. You and your sibling play I-spy on the ride. The car is running low on gas a couple miles from Indy, so the family stops at a gas station. You and your sibling grab Icees and trail mix for your journey. Where should you go first?

Finally, the family arrives at the Gateway Arch. You first take some pictures outside. Then, you enter the underground museum. After exploring the museum some, you, your sibling, and your dad get in line for the elevator. Your mom is afraid of heights, so she stays underground and learns more in the museum. The rest of the family waits for an hour to go up the elevator. You go up and admire the city below. When you get back down, you rejoin your mom, excited. You leave the museum. Next is the city museum!

The family arrives at the City Museum. You go in and buy tickets. Then, you set off to have fun and agree to meet back at noon if you get separated. The family wanders through tunnels, slides, staircase, and more. Filled with joy, you meet back at noon. You head out to lunch. Your dad find a brewery with delicious burgers. Next is the Gateway Arch!

You approach the Indianapolis Zoo. You enter the zoo and pay for tickets. You visit the aquatic animals first. Next, you head to the desert section. You enjoyed the Giant Tortoise exhibit. Next came the plains. Your mom's favorite exhibit, the cheetah, is in this section. The birds are next. The family then stops for lunch before visiting the forest area. Your sibling's favorite animal, the macaw, is in that section. Your dad cannot decide his favorite. The forest is the last section, so the family leaves after that. Next is the Soldiers and Sailors Monument!

The family drives into the center of downtown Indy. After parking, you walk to the Soldiers and Sailors Monument. The family takes several pictures and gazes at the architecture. Next, you walk around downtown, going into a couple stores. You eat at Cafe Patachou for lunch. Then you are ready for the next stop: the zoo!
